
## Page Breakdown

### index.html - Single Page Portfolio
**Purpose**: Complete portfolio with bio and full article archive

**Sections**:
1. **Header**
   - Site title
   - Tagline

2. **About Section**
   - Professional bio (3 paragraphs)
   - Research Interests (left column)
   - Professional Background (right column)

3. **Works Archive**
   - All 18 articles displayed
   - Each shows: category, date, title, publication, excerpt
   - Click interaction for article preview

4. **Footer**
   - Copyright information

## Interactive Features
### Scroll Animations
- Articles fade in with subtle upward motion as user scrolls
- Smooth transitions on hover effects

### Article Preview
- Clicking any article shows alert with title (placeholder for future article pages)

## Content Strategy
### Complete Article Archive (18 items)
- Categories: Research (5), Articles (5), Essays (5), Reviews (3)
- Date range: 2022-2024
- Realistic academic and professional titles/excerpts

## Technical Implementation
### Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Subtle text animations
- **Tailwind CSS**: Utility-first styling

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach
- Clean typography scaling
- Fast loading with minimal dependencies
